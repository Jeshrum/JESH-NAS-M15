If the trade is still open and TP was not hit → close it manually at market price.

## Rules for Following Signals

1. **Never risk more than 1–2% per trade.** If you risk 5%, one bad week wipes you.
2. **Always set SL and TP before walking away.** No exceptions.
3. **Never move your SL** to avoid a loss. That is how small losses become big ones.
4. **Take every signal.** Skipping signals because of recent losses breaks the edge.
5. **Force close at 4:30 PM Lagos** if the trade is still open.
6. **If you miss a signal, skip that day.** Do not enter late.
7. **Do not trade on heavy US news days** (red events at 2:30 PM Lagos on forexfactory.com).
